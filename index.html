How does it work?

Chordloops uses an algorithm to randomly arrange chords—incorporating voice leading, rhythm generation, and key and instrument selection. This produces a wide range of musical possibilities, resulting in many great-sounding loops!

Why use Chordloops.net?

It can inspire new songs, compositions, improvisations, and beats. Additionally, it is an effective ear trainer (scroll for lessons), an interactive game that provides endless practice for transcribing chord progressions.

Features

Supported are: Major and minor keys, borrowed chords, secondary dominants, and several possible variations of any chord (extensions, suspensions). Download audio (.wav) and MIDI for loops in Experiment Mode.
